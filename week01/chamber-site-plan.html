Enugu State Chamber of Commerce

Our mission (site purpose)

Enugu State Chamber of commerce mission is creating an environment in which businesses can prosper. Our goal is to promote interest in local business possibilities.Careful study is made of the community needs and an action plan is designed. The goal of the Action Plan is to improve the economic welfare of the community. The Chamber works for industrial and business development, which supports new residents with their revenues for our community. It provides educational opportunities and assists businesses with the latest marketing and promotional techniques.
